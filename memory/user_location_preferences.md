## Strong Preference: Deep Water Locations

User prefers **deep water locations** with focused power fishing over shallow/diverse river systems.

**Favorites so far:**
- **Saint-Croix Lake** - top favorite, deep lake with monster catfish/sturgeon, focused power fishing
- **White Moose Lake** - also enjoyed, deep water focus

**Did not enjoy:**
- **San Joaquin Delta** - shallow river, required diversified loadout for challenges, felt like compromise rather than optimization. Successfully completed challenges but not a return destination. UPDATE 4/13: Actually valuable for sturgeon income farming (300 lb in 5 hours at Fishing the Beaver) - revised to SECONDARY DESTINATION for sturgeon focus.
- **Tiber River (4/12)** - European river, very low fish density. 1 hit per 4-10 casts on active lures. Fish finder showed only a few fish every 2-3 min. 17 in-game hours for only 21k credits = ~1/3 of Saint-Croix rate. Travel cost 7,500 does NOT justify the trip. Completed Exp II on 4/13.
- **Lesní Víla Fishery (4/12)** - Czech panfish pond. Completed Exploration I-II but low excitement. Not a return destination after missions done.

**Mixed feelings:**
- **Kaniq Creek (4/13 PM)** - Alaska salmon river. Technical finesse fishing with multi-stage zig-zag spoon retrieve (smooth, not jerky; salmon hit on drop). Medium Spoon 6/7 Oz #6/0 = MVP. Caught Unique Chum 30.78 lb + Unique Sockeye 15.5 lb first session. XP/income underwhelming vs St-Croix and SJD. Tabled for "later date" - not immediate return. Good for mission completion, not income farming.
- **Sander Baggersee Lake (4/14 AM)** - German carp lake. Atmosphere is INDUSTRIAL (nuclear cooling towers, construction sites) - realistic but not scenic. Carp farming yielded only $2,734 / 444 XP in 1 hour = 1/10 of Saint-Croix rate. Bighead competition not pursued. Skipped the return trip; stashed carp rods. NOT a return destination.

## Why deep water locations are preferred
- Focused power fishing strategy (3+ heavy bottom rods + active lure rod)
- Big fish that justify X-Heavy gear investment
- Consistent volume from a single bait/setup combo (catfish on Medium Cutbait, sturgeon on Duck Mussel Meat/Large Cutbait)
- Less need to constantly swap rods and tackle
- More immersive than darting between tackle types

## Play style notes
- Prefers volume sessions filling the keepnet with big fish
- Enjoys completing expedition/challenge missions but at locations they enjoy
- Will accept lower $/hour rates at favorite locations vs higher-income locations they don't enjoy
- Travel cost matters - multiple round trips to retool loadout = significant profit erosion
- Prefers night fishing potential for sturgeon/catfish bonus opportunities

## How to apply
- When recommending locations, prioritize deep water lakes with monster fish
- For SJD return trips, plan single optimized loadout - no diversification
- Don't suggest finesse/light tackle locations as primary destinations
- DO mention valuable locations they don't prefer (with caveat about preference) for completeness, but expect they may skip
