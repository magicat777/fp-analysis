## Sturgeon Intel
- **Feed times:** 7-8 AM and 5-9 PM only
- **Location:** 50-100 ft in front of the smaller vessel's bow (vessel oriented lakeward), near Fairytale Docks
- **Technique:** Cast ~150 ft from dock corner, use rod pod, DO NOT yank to set hook — gentle pull and slow reel, keep tension at 80-90%, let it tire
- **Line requirement:** 50 lb broke on hook-set; use heavier line or gentle technique with 45-51 lb setups
