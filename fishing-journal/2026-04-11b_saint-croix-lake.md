## Zeus 8'10" SE vs. Salmonster 8'10" — Field Test
- **Verdict: negligible performance difference** between the two rods
- **Salmonster felt slightly easier to fight fish** — likely due to the **reel pairing**, not the rod itself:
  - Zeus 8'10" SE paired with **ThunderSpin 5500 SE** (5500-class)
  - Salmonster 8'10" paired with **Kraken 8000 SE** (8000-class — bigger spool, stronger drag, smoother retrieve under load)
- **Theory:** the Kraken 8000 has more line pickup per rotation and heavier drag, making big-fish fights feel more controlled
- **Action:** consider swapping Zeus 8'10" SE to a larger spinning reel for next test — candidates:
  - **Kraken 8000 SE** (if freed up)
  - **Ambusher 9000 SE** (currently on Mokonzi — probably overweight for a spinning rod feel)
  - **FatBoySpin 6000 SE** — natural step up from 5500 without going overboard
  - **EspiraDoublePunch 6500 SE** — another 6500-class candidate

## What Worked
- **Narrow Spoon 3/4 Oz #5/0 = session MVP lure** — dominated smallmouth bass, walleye, and brown trout catches (20+ hits visible in keeper). May be displacing Triple Runner as the top active lure at Fairytale.
- **Large Cutbait + Hook #7/0-#8/0** = produced all three Unique Blue Catfish (70, 71.8, 77.1 lb) plus most of the 40-60 lb class
- **Medium Cutbait + #6/0** still reliable for the 25-35 lb cat volume
- **Duck Mussel Meat on Mokonzi feeder** → Lake Sturgeon 40-43 lb (confirmed consistency from prior sessions)
- **Humenus 11'1" (Hellish Fighter)** slotted in cleanly as a 4th heavy bottom rod — handled big cats with no issue
- **Wicked Viking 8'6" repurposed** as a medium-range cat/match rig with Armored Slider + Medium Cutbait — worked here because it's fishing bait, not float (sidesteps the Fairytale current problem)

## What Didn't Work / Observations
- **Zeus 8'10" SE + ThunderSpin 5500 SE** paired feel "lighter" than Salmonster + Kraken 8000 during fights — not worse, just less torque
- Fish keeper hit over-capacity again — should consider shorter sessions or more frequent returns at current yield rate

## Key Takeaways
1. **Zeus 8'10" SE is a sidegrade at this reel pairing** — the extra range is there, but the fight feel is softer than Salmonster + Kraken 8000. Reel upsize test is the next experiment.
2. **Narrow Spoon 3/4 Oz #5/0 may be the new top active lure at Saint-Croix** — displacing Triple Runner for pure hit volume on smaller species. Triple Runner still holds the crown for bigger fish and the drop-pop technique.
3. **Four-rod stand (Club Oasis) unlocks a legit 4x heavy-bottom cat farm** — next session should run Heavy Chaser + Humenus + Tribal Totem + Wicked Viking (or Mokonzi) simultaneously. That's the new power-fishing configuration.
4. **3 Unique Blue Catfish in one session is extraordinary** — Fairytale Docks + Large Cutbait remains the proven unique factory.
5. **Level 35 → 36 is only 6,180 XP away** — one more solid session (or two shorter ones) and the Muskie Golem mission unlocks.
