## What Worked — Big Findings

### Zeus 8'2" NE Spinning + Triple Runner (MVP of the trip)
- **Excellent responsiveness** with Triple Runner 8 ft #5/0 for smaller active species
- **Drop-jig technique nailed down:** drop to bottom → pause 4 sec → raise to level + swim 4 sec → repeat
- **Aggressive bottom fish (bass, walleye) love the popup from rest** — most hits came on the raise or the drop from pause
- **Almost all smaller fish in the keeper came from this method**
- Perfect complement to the 3 heavy bottom rods — covers the "active" slot that Salmonster used to hold

### Bait/Hook Optimization on Bottom Rigs (Confirmed)
- **Medium Cutbait + #6/0** = top producer for Blue Catfish (re-confirmed from 4/8)
- **Large Cutbait + #8/0** = solid secondary, especially on the trophy-class fish
- **Larger hooks (#9/0+) = less production** — fish refuse or get unhooked
- **Alternate baits (Dried Fish Heads, Duck Mussel Meat) = notably less productive** than cutbait on this trip
- Sturgeon still hit Duck Mussel Meat and Medium Cutbait but bites were slower

## What Didn't Work / Stashed

### WBT BassEnchanter 7'3" Casting (from template)
- **Earned its name** — the rod is legit and hits hard
- **Range too short** for Fairytale Docks — couldn't reach the sweet spots where fish were stacked
- Stayed in the backpack after a few test casts

### Wicked Viking 8'6" Match Rod
- **Shorter range issue** similar to BassEnchanter
- **Setup felt wrong for Saint-Croix** — this rod is clearly designed for deep ocean/sea fishing
- **Fairytale current problem:** moderate current flows *toward* the dock, pulling float rigs in and out of target range quickly. Float fishing here is frustrating for that reason alone.
- Stashed for now — will reconsider at a true deep-water spot or save for saltwater locations

## Key Takeaways
1. **Zeus 8'2" NE is the new #2 slot** — replaces any soft-bait jig setup. Pair with Triple Runner 8 ft #5/0 and use the drop/pause/raise cadence. This is now the proven active-lure rig for Fairytale.
2. **Drop-and-pop cadence** works specifically because Fairytale Docks bottom fish are sitting deep and ambush the rise from rest. Worth trying this cadence at other deep spots too.
3. **Medium Cutbait + #6/0** is the established best cat/sturgeon combo. Large Cutbait + #8/0 is the backup. Stop experimenting with alternate baits here — they underperform.
4. **Float fishing is fundamentally broken at Fairytale** due to the dock-ward current. Skip all match rod plans for this spot; move to deeper water or a different location if float is needed.
5. **Shorter-range rods (BassEnchanter, Wicked Viking) don't work at Fairytale** — the productive zone is beyond their casting distance. Stick with longer bottom rods and a long-range spinning rod.
6. **Panfish Trophies 6/7** — only Trophy Oscar remains. That's an Everglades fish, not here.
