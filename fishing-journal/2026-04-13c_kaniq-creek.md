## 🎯 Kaniq Retrieve Technique (DOCUMENTED — new location-specific technique)
Multi-stage zig-zag retrieve for Pacific salmon — **smooth, NOT jerky**:

1. **Cast** to target zone
2. **Let drop SLOWLY** to bottom — (6/7 Oz spoon weight is key — heavier lures drop too fast)
3. **Reel + raise slowly** until lure reaches ~50% depth from bottom
4. **Let drop slowly** again
5. **Raise just before bottom** (~80-90% deep)
6. **Raise to ~40% depth**
7. **Drop slowly** again
8. Repeat the cycle until lure approaches surface
9. Final drop to ~70-80% depth, then retrieve

**Salmon hit almost exclusively on the DROP phase — rarely on the raise.**

**Smooth movement > twitches or jerks.** Jerky retrieves shut down the bite.
