## What Worked
- **Drop-and-pause technique on trout** (same cadence as Saint-Croix) — successfully completed the Tiber Trout mission by boating around and using this pattern
- **Barbless Nano Spinner 1/8 Oz #1/0** — MVP active lure for Tiber, produced trophies and uniques across chub, perch, grayling, marble trout
- **Spinner 1/8 Oz #1/0** — close second, trophy chub producer at evening
- **Medium Cutbait** produced the Unique European Eel at night
- **Troy 23' telescopic** covered all the Euro panfish baits (Pinkies, Bloodworms, Red Worms, Semolina, Bread with Honey)
- **Variety coverage** — 4 bottom rods with different baits/hooks did catch all required species eventually

## What Didn't Work (User Assessment)
- **Tiber River felt SLOW** — 1 hit per 4-10 casts on spin casting rods
- **Panfishing was slow despite 4 bottom rods** — long waits between hits even with diverse bait/hook combos
- **Fish density low** — fish finder showed only a few fish every 2-3 minutes
- **17 in-game hours for 21k credits** = ~1/3 of Saint-Croix's hourly income rate
- **Travel cost ~7,500 does NOT justify the trip** at this income level
- **Boat trolling was the workaround** — had to actively move to find fish for the trout mission
- **Right gear, wrong density** — setups were correct, the location just doesn't have the Saint-Croix concentration

## Key Takeaways
1. **Euro river locations are LOW-DENSITY compared to US deep lakes** — even with the right setup, hit rates are 1/4 to 1/10 of Saint-Croix
2. **Mission completion is the only reason to visit** — one-time Exploration + Fish Monster payoffs, not profit farming
3. **7,500 credit travel cost is a real tax** — at Tiber's density, you need 30+k in fish to even break even after supplies
4. **Telescopic rod confirmed useful for completion runs** — Troy 23' + Pinkies caught panfish efficiently
5. **Trophy/Unique catches are still worth the trip** — Unique Eel, Unique Chub, Unique Perch added to career total
6. **Barbless Nano Spinner 1/8 Oz #1/0** and **Spinner 1/8 Oz #1/0** are the go-to active lures at Tiber if you do return
7. **Saint-Croix remains the income king** — any "variety" trip has to be evaluated against the opportunity cost of a Saint-Croix session instead
